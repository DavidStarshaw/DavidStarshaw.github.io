Curriculum links

This standard is linked to level 7 of the New Zealand Curriculum. It links to the level 7 "equations and expressions" achievement objectives:

  • M 7-6 Manipulate rational, exponential, and logarithmic algebraic expressions
  • M 7-7 Form and use linear, quadratic, and simple trigonometric equations
