Standard
Assessment specifications

This standard is linked to levels 5 and 6 of the New Zealand Curriculum.

Level 5

  • S 5-2 Evaluate statistical investigations or probability activities undertaken by others, including data collection methods, choice of measures, and validity of findings
  • S 5-4 Calculate probabilities, using fractions, percentages, and ratios
Level 6
  • S 6-2 Evaluate statistical reports in the media by relating the displays, statistics, processes, and probabilities used to the claims made
  • S 6-3 Investigate situations that involve elements of chance: calculating probabilities in discrete situations
