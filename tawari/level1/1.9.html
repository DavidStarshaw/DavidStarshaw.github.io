This standard is linked to levels 5 and 6 of the New Zealand Curriculum. It links to the achievement objectives:

Level 5

  • GM 5-9 Define and use transformations and describe the invariant properties of figures and objects under these transformations.
Level 6
  • GM 6-8 Compare and apply single and multiple transformations
  • GM 6-9 Analyse symmetrical patterns by the transformations used to create them
