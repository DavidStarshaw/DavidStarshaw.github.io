Summary

This morning you are introduced to the compost unit and what you can expect. You will be introduced to the current compost system in Kawakawa and the work of previous students. You will, as a group, start one new compost pile to see and feel how it works. Then you'll begin to design an experiment with your own compost pile to test the best methods of composting. This experiment will run during the entire unit (two weeks).
