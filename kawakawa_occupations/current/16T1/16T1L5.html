Summary

This morning you'll solve a problem. Your compost needs to be fed. But it won't be yours forever and it'll still need to be fed and cared for. As a group, put your minds together to come up with a system or a process that the whole of Kawakawa can follow to keep the compost piles effective and useful.

Reflection and debrief
